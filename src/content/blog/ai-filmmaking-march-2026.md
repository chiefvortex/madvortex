- **Runway Gen-4**: Best quality + character persistence across scenes.

- **Google Veo 3.1**: Deep integration with Drive and YouTube Studio.
- **Seedance 2.0**: Highest Elo score in blind tests. China-only until Q2.

## Frontier Models

- **GPT-5.4**: 1.05 million token context window. Scored 83% on GDPVal.

- **NVIDIA Nemotron 3 Super**: 120B total parameters, 12B active per forward pass.

- **Midjourney V8**: Completely rewritten engine. 5x faster, native 2K output.
- **Flux 2**: Photorealism king. Skin textures that fool people in blind tests.
- **Ideogram v3**: The only tool that reliably renders text inside images.
- **Adobe Firefly 4**: IP indemnification included. The safe commercial choice.

The pitch isn't "AI is cool" anymore. It's "production infrastructure using tools that just hit maturity this month." Multi-shot consistency + AI dubbing + real-time rendering = a real service offering, not a demo reel.
